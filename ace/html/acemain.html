Welcome to ACE IT EDUCATION

ACE IT EDUCATION center is managed by experts in the field of Computer Training who prides itself for providing hands-on practicals computer training to both individuals as well as corporate professionals. ACE IT EDUCATION Center is your one stop center for exceptional training programs in a wide variety of delivery formats including computer based training, hands-on computer courses. ACE understands the need of everyone and focus on the importance of the teaching methodology. It has a preferred method of learning system. Keeping in the mind ACE has developed its own training cum teaching tools to use in the class rooms and make an effective learning. It prepare students by delivering Hands-on for its job oriented courses which teaches them how to apply the knowledge in the field of IT work atmosphere. It is all about industry need basis and student centric Institute.

ACE has partnered with Microsoft, CompTIA and offer wide variety of training courses that includes Computer Hardware(A+), Computer Networking(N+) Microsoft Certifications (MCTS/MCITP), CCNA and Linux Certificates. Apart from the above courses ACE trains students in Computer Basics and software related courses like MS Word, Excel, Power Point & Tally. ACE has focused on English language course to prepare the students in developing their communication skills to make them industry ready to face IT jobs.

ACE also provides testing facility for international certifications by its Pearson VUE Authorised Testing Center, like A+ CompTIA Certification, Microsoft Certifications, CISCO Certifications and other certifications.
